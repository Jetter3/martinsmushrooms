Mushroom Recipes

Shiitake Mushroom Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shiitake mushrooms, stems removed and sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. Add onion and bell pepper, cook until softened.
  4. Add shiitake mushrooms and soy sauce, cook until mushrooms are tender.
  5. Serve over cooked rice and enjoy!

Oyster Mushroom Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb oyster mushrooms, shredded
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Corn tortillas
  • Optional toppings: diced onion, cilantro, lime wedges, salsa

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add shredded oyster mushrooms and spices, cook until mushrooms are crispy and golden.
  4. Warm tortillas in a separate pan.
  5. Assemble tacos with mushrooms and desired toppings.

Grilled Portobello Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms, stems removed
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix balsamic vinegar, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Brush the mixture onto both sides of the portobello mushrooms.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill mushrooms for 4-5 minutes on each side, until tender and slightly charred.
  5. Serve as a main dish or on a sandwich.
